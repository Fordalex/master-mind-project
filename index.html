Master Mind

How To Play

Aim Of The Game

Work out what sequence counters that Mastermind has hidden under cover.

Step: 1

Place your first 4 counters by clicking on the counters to the side of the board, Then press 'check'.

Step: 2

If you got any coloured counters right, the computer will return black and white pegs.

Step: 3

you have 10 attempts or the computer will win!
